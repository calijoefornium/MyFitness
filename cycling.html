CYCLING


❮ ❯

Equipment:

bike(bicycle) , helmet

Benefits:

The health benefits of regular cycling include:

  • increased cardiovascular fitness.
  • increased muscle strength and flexibility.
  • improved joint mobility.
  • decreased stress levels.
  • improved posture and coordination.
  • strengthened bones.
  • decreased body fat levels.
  • prevention or management of disease

Safety:

Walk in well-lit and populated areas. Choose shoes that offer good ankle support to reduce your risk for injury.

Duration and frequency:

running:30 to 60 minutes, 4 to 5 times per week

If you're a beginner:

Pump your tyres to the right pressure , Get your saddle height and riding position just right , Dress for the conditions , Take water and food , Padded shorts , Cycling gloves and mitts , Take a lock and lights (just in case) , Have a saddlepack with tools, spares and cash
